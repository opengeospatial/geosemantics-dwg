The purpose of this SWG is to revise, publish, and maintain standard methods for referencing and maintaining terminology resources across OGC standards.

These methods include schema patterns, profile descriptions, validations, and service APIs.

Incorporation of SWG outputs into other standards may be through direct dependencies or through publications of profiles for best practices.
